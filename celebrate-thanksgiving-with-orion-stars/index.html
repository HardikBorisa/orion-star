Thanksgiving on “Friends”

The “Friends” series is renowned for its annual Thanksgiving-themed episodes, and Chandler played a pivotal role in numerous memorable scenes.

Let’s revisit a few iconic Thanksgiving episodes that showcased Chandler’s unique approach to the holiday.

The One with the Underdog Gets Away (Season 1, Episode 7):

In this episode, Chandler finds himself spending Thanksgiving away from his family and tries to cheer up Joey, who is also feeling down. Chandler’s blend of sarcasm and compassion sets the tone for future Thanksgiving episodes.

The One with Chandler in a Box (Season 4, Episode 8):

In this classic episode, Chandler ends up confined in a wooden crate as a form of penance for kissing Kathy, Joey’s girlfriend. His challenging situations often provided a welcome source of humor, illustrating his readiness to go to great lengths for reconciliation.

The One with All the Thanksgivings (Season 5, Episode 8):

This episode takes viewers on a nostalgic journey to various Thanksgiving dinners from previous years. Chandler’s unique humor and heartfelt moments with Monica add depth to his character.

The One with Chandler and Monica’s Wedding (Season 7, Episode 23/24):

While not a traditional Thanksgiving episode, Chandler’s wedding to Monica marks a significant moment in the series. It illustrates the growth of his character and underscores the importance of love and relationships during the holiday season.
